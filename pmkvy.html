Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

Objectives

The Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) aims to provide industry-relevant skill training to youth, thereby improving their employability and promoting skill development in India. The scheme also aims to recognize and certify skills gained through informal training channels.

Benefits

Under the PMKVY scheme, the following benefits are provided:

  • Free skill training in various industry-relevant courses.
  • Certification upon successful completion of training and assessment.
  • Placement assistance and support for entrepreneurial endeavors.
  • Monetary rewards to trainees upon certification.

Eligibility

The scheme is targeted at the following groups:

  • Indian nationals who are unemployed or school/college dropouts.
  • Candidates who have undergone training in courses aligned with the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF).
  • Individuals who have a valid Aadhaar Card and a bank account.

Application Process

To apply for the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official PMKVY website or the nearest training center.
  2. Register for the desired skill training course by filling out the application form.
  3. Submit the required documents for verification.
  4. Attend the training sessions as per the schedule provided by the training center.
  5. Undergo assessment and certification upon completion of the training program.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
