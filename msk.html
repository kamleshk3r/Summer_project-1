Mahila Shakti Kendra (MSK)

Objectives

MSK aims to empower rural women through community participation and gender-sensitive interventions.

Components

  • Community awareness on issues affecting women.
  • Capacity building for economic and social empowerment.
  • Facilitation of access to entitlements.
  • Support services for violence-affected women.
  • Coordination and convergence among stakeholders.

Eligibility

Women in rural areas are eligible to benefit from MSK initiatives.

Application Process

Interested women can participate through self-help groups (SHGs) or community organizations affiliated with MSK.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Mahila Shakti Kendra (MSK) scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
