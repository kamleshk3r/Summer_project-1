National Nutrition Mission (POSHAN Abhiyaan)

Objectives

POSHAN Abhiyaan aims to reduce malnutrition among children (0-6 years), adolescent girls, pregnant women, and lactating mothers.

Components

  • ICDS-CAS for Real-Time Monitoring.
  • Convergence and community mobilization.
  • Jan Andolan for improved nutrition practices.
  • Intensive implementation in high-burden districts.

Eligibility

Children (0-6 years), adolescent girls, pregnant women, and lactating mothers are eligible to benefit from POSHAN Abhiyaan.

Application Process

Families can enroll in POSHAN Abhiyaan through Anganwadi centers or designated health and nutrition services.
