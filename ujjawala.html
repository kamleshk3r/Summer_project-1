UJJAWALA Scheme

Objectives

The UJJAWALA Scheme aims to prevent trafficking of women and children for commercial sexual exploitation, and to facilitate their rescue and rehabilitation.

Components

  • Prevention.
  • Rescue.
  • Rehabilitation.
  • Reintegration.
  • Repatriation.

Eligibility

Victims of trafficking for commercial sexual exploitation and those at risk are eligible for support under the UJJAWALA Scheme.

Application Process

Victims can seek assistance through government-recognized institutions and NGOs providing support under the scheme.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Ujjawala scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
