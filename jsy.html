Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)

Objectives

The primary objective of the Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) is to reduce maternal and neonatal mortality by promoting institutional delivery among poor pregnant women.

Benefits

Under the JSY scheme, the following benefits are provided:

  • Cash assistance to pregnant women for institutional delivery.
  • Post-delivery care for the mother and neonatal care for the newborn.
  • Encouragement to opt for institutional deliveries to ensure safer childbirth.

Eligibility

The scheme is targeted at the following groups:

  • Pregnant women from low-income families.
  • Women aged 19 years or above.
  • Women belonging to the Below Poverty Line (BPL) category or Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe (SC/ST).

Application Process

To apply for the Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY), follow these steps:

  1. Visit the nearest government health facility or accredited private health facility.
  2. Fill out the application form for JSY.
  3. Submit the necessary documents, including proof of pregnancy, identity proof, and BPL/SC/ST certificate.
  4. Ensure that you attend the required antenatal check-ups and deliver the baby at an accredited facility.
  5. After delivery, submit the birth certificate of the newborn to receive the cash assistance.
