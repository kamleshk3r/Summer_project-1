National Scheme of Incentive to Girls for Secondary Education (NSIGSE)

Objectives

The objective of NSIGSE is to promote enrollment of girl children in secondary education and ensure their retention.

Benefits

  • An amount of Rs. 3,000 is deposited in the name of eligible girls as a fixed deposit, which can be withdrawn along with interest on reaching 18 years of age.

Eligibility

  • All SC/ST girls who pass class VIII and enroll in class IX in State Government, Government-aided, and local body schools.
  • Girls who have completed 14 years of age and are enrolled in class IX in the same year.

Application Process

The scheme is implemented through the respective State Governments/UT Administrations. Eligible girls need to apply through their school authorities.

More Information

For more detailed information about the National Scheme of Incentive to Girls for Secondary Education (NSIGSE) scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
