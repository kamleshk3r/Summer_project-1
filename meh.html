Mahila E-Haat

Objectives

Mahila E-Haat is an online marketing platform to encourage and support women entrepreneurs, artisans, and women SHGs by facilitating online selling of their products.

Key Features

  • Platform for women to showcase and sell their products online.
  • Promotes financial independence among women entrepreneurs.
  • Supports rural and urban women artisans and SHGs.

Eligibility

Women entrepreneurs, artisans, and SHGs can participate in Mahila E-Haat.

Application Process

Interested women can register and list their products on the Mahila E-Haat portal.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Mahila E-Haat (MEH) scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
