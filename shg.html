Self Help Groups (SHGs) under National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM)

Objectives

The NRLM promotes SHGs to enable women to become self-reliant by improving their access to financial services and livelihood opportunities.

Components

  • Formation and nurturing of SHGs.
  • Access to microfinance and bank credit.
  • Capacity building and skill enhancement.
  • Promotion of livelihoods and enterprise development.
  • Social mobilization and institution building.

Eligibility

Women from rural households, especially poor and marginalized communities, are eligible to form and benefit from SHGs under NRLM.

Application Process

Women can join existing SHGs or form new ones through community-based organizations and NRLM-supported programs.

More Information
