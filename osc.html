One Stop Centre Scheme (OSC)

Objectives

The objective of the OSC scheme is to provide support and assistance to women affected by violence, both in private and public spaces.

Services Offered

  • Emergency response and rescue services.
  • Medical aid and assistance.
  • Legal counseling and support.
  • Psychological and counseling support.
  • Temporary shelter and rehabilitation.

Eligibility

All women affected by violence, including domestic violence, sexual assault, trafficking, etc., are eligible for services under OSC.

Application Process

Women can access OSC services through referral or by directly approaching the nearest One Stop Centre.

More Information

For more detailed information about the One Stop Center (OSC) scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
