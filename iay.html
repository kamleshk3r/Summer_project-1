Indira Awaas Yojana (IAY)

Objectives

IAY aimed to provide housing for the rural poor with a focus on BPL households, SCs, STs, freed bonded laborers, and minorities.

Components

  • Provision of financial assistance for house construction.
  • Basic amenities like toilets, smokeless chulhas, and drinking water facilities.
  • Infrastructure development for integrated housing projects.

Eligibility

BPL households identified through surveys conducted by local authorities are eligible for IAY.

Application Process

Applications were processed through Gram Panchayats or rural development authorities.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Indira Awas Yojana scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
