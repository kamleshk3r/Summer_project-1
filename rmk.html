Rashtriya Mahila Kosh (RMK)

Objectives

The primary objective of the Rashtriya Mahila Kosh (RMK) is to provide microfinance services to bring about the socio-economic upliftment of poor women, especially in rural areas, by promoting their participation in productive activities and income generation.

Benefits

The Rashtriya Mahila Kosh (RMK) scheme offers the following benefits:

  • Provision of micro-credit to poor women to support their income-generating activities.
  • Facilitation of access to credit for women's self-help groups (SHGs) and other groups.
  • Support for the establishment and expansion of small enterprises.
  • Encouragement of women's entrepreneurship and self-employment.

Eligibility

The Rashtriya Mahila Kosh (RMK) is targeted at the following groups:

  • Women belonging to economically weaker sections of society.
  • Women self-help groups (SHGs) and other groups engaged in income-generating activities.
  • Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) and other agencies working with women in rural and urban areas.

Application Process

To apply for assistance under the Rashtriya Mahila Kosh (RMK), follow these steps:

  1. Identify a suitable microfinance scheme offered by RMK that meets your needs.
  2. Contact the nearest RMK office or an approved intermediary organization for application forms and guidelines.
  3. Submit the completed application form along with the required documents.
  4. Ensure that all group members (in case of SHGs) meet the eligibility criteria and provide necessary information.
  5. Await the approval process and follow up with the RMK office or intermediary organization as required.
