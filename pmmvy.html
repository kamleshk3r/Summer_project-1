Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

Objectives

The primary objective of the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) is to provide partial wage compensation to pregnant women and lactating mothers to enable them to rest adequately during their pregnancy and after delivery, and to improve health-seeking behavior among them.

Benefits

Under the PMMVY scheme, the following benefits are provided:

  • Cash incentive of ₹5,000/- is provided in three installments.
  • The first installment of ₹1,000/- on early registration of pregnancy at the Anganwadi Centre/approved Health facility.
  • The second installment of ₹2,000/- after six months of pregnancy on receiving at least one antenatal check-up (ANC).
  • The third installment of ₹2,000/- after childbirth is registered and the child has received the first cycle of BCG, OPV, DPT, and Hepatitis-B, or its equivalent/substitute.

Eligibility

The scheme is targeted at the following groups:

  • Pregnant women and lactating mothers who are 19 years of age or above for the first live birth.
  • Women who are not in regular employment with the Central/State Government or a Public Sector Undertaking.
  • Women who have registered their pregnancy at the Anganwadi Centre (AWC) or approved health facility within 150 days of their last menstrual period (LMP).

Application Process

To apply for the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY), follow these steps:

  1. Register at the nearest Anganwadi Centre (AWC) or approved health facility.
  2. Fill out the application form for PMMVY and submit the required documents.
  3. Ensure that you follow the ANC schedule and submit the necessary documents at each stage to receive the installments.
  4. Submit the birth certificate of the newborn and immunization records to receive the final installment.
