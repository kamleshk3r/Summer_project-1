Mudra Yojana for Women

Objectives

Mudra Yojana aims to provide financial assistance to women entrepreneurs for starting or expanding their business ventures.

Loan Categories

  • Shishu: Loans up to ₹50,000.
  • Kishor: Loans from ₹50,001 to ₹5,00,000.
  • Tarun: Loans from ₹5,00,001 to ₹10,00,000.

Eligibility

Women entrepreneurs, including individuals, partnerships, or organizations, are eligible for Mudra loans.

Application Process

Applicants can apply for Mudra loans through participating banks or financial institutions.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Mudra Yojana scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
