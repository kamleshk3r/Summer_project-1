Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)

Objectives

The objective of SSY is to encourage parents to build a fund for the future education and marriage expenses of their girl child.

Benefits

  • High interest rate on deposits.
  • Income tax benefits under section 80C of the Income Tax Act.
  • Partial withdrawal facility after the girl reaches 18 years of age.

Eligibility

  • The scheme is available for a girl child up to 10 years of age.
  • Only one account per girl child is allowed, and a maximum of two accounts for a family.

Application Process

Parents or guardians can open an SSY account at any post office or authorized bank by submitting the required documents and the initial deposit amount.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
