Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP)

Objectives

The Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme aims to address the declining child sex ratio (CSR) and to ensure survival, protection, and education of the girl child. The objectives are to prevent gender-biased sex-selective elimination, ensure survival and protection of the girl child, and ensure education and participation of the girl child.

Benefits

The Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme offers the following benefits:

  • Increased awareness and improved efficiency of welfare services for women.
  • Better enforcement of laws and policies to protect the girl child.
  • Access to quality education and vocational training for girls.
  • Community mobilization and participation to create a positive environment for the girl child.

Eligibility

The Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme targets the following groups:

  • All girl children, especially those in districts with a low child sex ratio.
  • Communities and families in areas identified for gender-biased sex selection.
  • Government and non-government organizations working towards the welfare of the girl child.

Application Process

To benefit from the Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme, follow these steps:

  1. Check if your area is covered under the BBBP scheme by contacting local authorities or visiting the official website.
  2. Participate in awareness programs and community mobilization events organized under the BBBP scheme.
  3. Ensure the enrollment of girl children in schools and encourage their participation in educational and extracurricular activities.
  4. Contact the nearest BBBP office or designated authorities for any specific benefits or schemes available under BBBP.
