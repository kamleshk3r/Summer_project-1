Stand-Up India Scheme

Objectives

The Stand-Up India Scheme aims to promote entrepreneurship among women, SCs, and STs by facilitating bank loans and support for setting up greenfield enterprises.

Key Features

  • Collateral-free loans for setting up greenfield enterprises.
  • Focus on economic empowerment of women and marginalized communities.
  • Supports entrepreneurship through financial assistance and handholding.

Eligibility

SC/ST and women entrepreneurs are eligible for loans under this scheme.

Application Process

Applicants can apply through participating banks or online portals designated for Stand-Up India.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Stand-Up India scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
