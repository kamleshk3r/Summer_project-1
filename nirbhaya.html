Nirbhaya Fund

Objectives

The Nirbhaya Fund aims to support initiatives that enhance the safety and security of women in India.

Utilization

  • Setting up helplines and crisis response teams.
  • Creating safe city projects.
  • Supporting initiatives for women's safety in public transport.
  • Strengthening forensic facilities.
  • Supporting victim compensation schemes.

Eligibility

Government agencies, NGOs, and institutions working towards women's safety and empowerment are eligible to apply for funding under the Nirbhaya Fund.

Application Process

Interested organizations can apply through the Ministry of Women and Child Development or other designated authorities.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Nirbhaya Fund scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
