Swadhar Greh

Objectives

Swadhar Greh aims to provide temporary shelter and support services to women in difficult circumstances.

Services Offered

  • Temporary shelter.
  • Counseling and emotional support.
  • Rehabilitation services.
  • Skill development and training.
  • Legal aid and support.

Eligibility

Women above 18 years of age, without any age limit, who are in distress and have no social and economic support, are eligible for Swadhar Greh.

Application Process

Women can approach the nearest Swadhar Greh or contact the implementing agency for admission and support.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Swadhar Greh scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
