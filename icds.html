Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS)

Objectives

ICDS aims to improve the health, nutrition, and education of children under six years of age, as well as pregnant and lactating mothers.

Services Offered

  • Supplementary nutrition.
  • Immunization.
  • Health check-ups.
  • Referral services.
  • Early childhood care and education (ECCE).

Eligibility

Children under six years of age, pregnant women, and lactating mothers are eligible to receive services under ICDS.

Application Process

Families can approach the nearest Anganwadi center or ICDS office to enroll and avail of services.

More Information

For more detailed information about the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
