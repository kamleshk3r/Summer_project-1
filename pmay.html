Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Objectives

PMAY aims to provide affordable housing to all eligible beneficiaries by the year 2022.

Components

  • In-situ rehabilitation of existing slum dwellers.
  • Credit-linked subsidy scheme.
  • Affordable housing in partnership.
  • Subsidy for beneficiary-led individual house construction or enhancement.

Eligibility

Various income groups, including economically weaker sections (EWS), lower income groups (LIG), and middle income groups (MIG), are eligible based on specific criteria.

Application Process

Beneficiaries can apply through online portals or at designated Common Service Centres (CSCs).

More Information

For more detailed information about the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana scheme, you can visit the official website or contact the helpline.
